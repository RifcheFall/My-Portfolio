About us

CAFALL Charity Is A Nonprofit Organization Dedicated To Making A Positive Impact, Particularly In Africa. Our Mission Is To Improve The Lives Of Those In Need By Providing Access To Essential Resources, Education, Sports, And More. We Believe That Everyone Deserves The Opportunity To Live A Healthy, Fulfilling Life, And We Are Committed To Making This Vision A Reality. Our Team Consists Of Passionate Individuals United By A Shared Goal: To Make A Meaningful Difference In The Lives Of Others. With Dedication And Compassion, We Strive To Enhance The Well-Being Of Individuals And Communities. We Collaborate With A Network Of Partners And Supporters Who Share Our Vision And Values. Together, We Work To Achieve Our Goals, Leveraging Collective Strengths And Resources To Bring About Positive Change.Your Support Is Crucial To Our Success. Whether Through Donations, Volunteer Work, Or Partnerships, Every Contribution Helps Us Make A Lasting Impact. We Invite You To Join Us In Our Efforts To Create A Better World For All.

Our Goal

At CAFALL Charity, Our Mission Is To Enhance Lives And Create Positive Change. To Guide Our Efforts, We've Outlined Key Goals: Educational Support: Provide School Supplies To Families In Need, Ensuring Children Have Essential Tools For Learning. Sports Outreach: Distribute Basketball Shoes And Sports Equipment To Underserved Individuals, Promoting Physical Activity And Well-Being. Education Initiatives: Support Educational Programs In Africa, Focusing On Building Schools, Providing Materials, And Fostering Literacy In Senegal. Healthcare Access: Offer Medical Support To Underserved Communities, Improving Access To Vital Services And Health Outcomes. Environmental Stewardship: Advocate For Environmental Protection And Sustainable Practices To Reduce Waste And Combat Climate Change. Social Justice And Equality: Champion Social Justice, Advocate For Marginalized Groups, And Combat Discrimination To Create A More Inclusive Society. We're Committed To Achieving These Goals And Making A Lasting Impact. Your Support Helps Us Advance Our Mission And Create A Better Future For All.

Our Impact



First Backpacks Giveaway in 2018.

CAFALL Charity organized its first backpack giveaway, providing essential school supplies to children in need. This initiative made a significant impact, not only equipping students with the tools they needed for learning but also alleviating financial burdens for families struggling to afford these supplies.

The backpacks were warmly received by the children and their families, fostering a sense of hope and empowerment within the community. This successful event underscored the importance of our mission and inspired us to continue our efforts to support education and enhance the lives of those in need.

Shoes Give Away AT SEBI Basket Club.

CAFALL Charity partnered with Sebi Basket Club to distribute basketball shoes to underserved individuals in Senegal. This initiative aimed to promote physical activity, enhance well-being, and provide essential support to aspiring athletes. The basketball shoes not only improved access to sports equipment but also inspired a sense of community and unity among the recipients. This collaboration underscored the power of sports as a tool for positive change, highlighting the importance of collective efforts in making a meaningful impact.

SHOES GIVE AWAY AT POUT BASKET CLUB

CAFALL Charity partnered with POUT Basket Club to organize a basketball shoe giveaway initiative. This initiative provided new and gently-used basketball shoes to underserved individuals, allowing them to engage in sports and physical activity with proper footwear. The donation of these basketball shoes not only promoted physical well-being but also fostered a sense of community and unity through sports. This collaborative effort exemplified the positive impact that collective action can have in empowering individuals and enriching communities.
